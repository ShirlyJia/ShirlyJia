## About Me

I'm an MSc FinTech student at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore. My current focus is learning how to turn financial scenarios, user needs, and data signals into practical product decisions.

I am especially interested in the bridge between product management and technical execution: defining the right problem, understanding users and business constraints, working with data, and translating ideas into clear product requirements or prototypes.

## Product Lens

| Area | How I Think About It |
| --- | --- |
| User Problems | Clarify who the user is, what job they need done, and where the current workflow breaks. |
| Financial Context | Connect product choices with trust, risk, compliance, usability, and measurable value. |
| AI Products | Look for use cases where AI improves decisions, automation, personalization, or workflow efficiency. |
| Data & Metrics | Use data to validate assumptions, prioritize trade-offs, and define success criteria. |
| Execution | Turn ambiguous ideas into requirements, workflows, prototypes, and readable documentation. |
